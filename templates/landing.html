noBot

You say no. It says it better.

What is it?

noBot is a JoystickTV chat bot that listens for you — or your moderators — saying "no" in your own chat. When it hears a plain refusal (nope, nah, hell no, absolutely not, etc.), it immediately fires a funnier, more creative version of "no" into chat on your behalf. Think of it as your chat's resident professional no-sayer.

It also gives you a private web panel with a big red No button. Press it any time — live on stream, away from chat, as an OBS dock — and noBot drops a phrase into chat as if it just felt like saying no all on its own.

How does it work?

Step 1 — Install

Click Install noBot below and authorize it through JoystickTV. Takes about 30 seconds. noBot immediately starts watching your chat.

Step 2 — Say no

You or a mod types something like "no", "nope", "nah", "absolutely not", or "hell no" in your chat. noBot responds with a randomly selected, funnier take on the same sentiment. It only triggers on short, standalone refusals — not when "no" appears naturally in longer sentences.

Step 3 — Or just press the button

You get a private panel page with a big red button labeled No. Press it whenever you want noBot to fire — regardless of chat. Add the page as an OBS browser source dock so the button sits inside your streaming setup.

Chat commands

Only you and your moderators can use these:

!nobot off Disable chat-triggered responses for the rest of this stream.
!nobot on Re-enable them mid-stream. noBot is on by default whenever you go live.
!nobothelp noBot whispers you the link to your private panel.

The on/off toggle applies only to chat triggers. The button on your panel always works, regardless of toggle state.

Privacy

noBot only reads chat messages from your channel and only acts on short, standalone "no"-type phrases from you or your mods. It does not store message history, viewer data, or anything beyond what is needed to keep your session running. Your panel is protected by a private URL — only people you share it with can press the button.

Install noBot

You'll be taken to JoystickTV to authorize. After approving, you'll land on your private panel with setup instructions. Free to use.

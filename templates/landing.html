noBot

For every "no" you say in your own chat, noBot finds a funnier one.

What it does

  • Watches your chat for you or your mods saying "no" (or nope, nah, hell no, etc.).
  • When it hears a simple refusal, it drops a funnier variation into chat.
  • Gives you a private web panel with a big red No button you can press on demand — or add as an OBS dock.
  • Toggle off for a stream with !nobot off; resets to on when you go live again.
  • !nobothelp whispers you the link to your panel.

Install noBot

You'll be sent to JoystickTV to authorize. After approving, you'll be redirected to your panel.
